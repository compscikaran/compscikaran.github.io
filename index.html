Hi, I am Karan Gupta!

I am a budding software engineer and enthusiast. I constantly play around with new technologies and dabble in open source software.

You have to not only look at the higher level design but also the lower level implementation details. It's part art and part science and thats what is so amazing about it.

I have worked on multiple projects both production and experimental and they have taughtg me about the intricacies of this trade. Taking inspiration from the software craftsman movement I try to make my code clean, maintainable and testable.
